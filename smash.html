Smash Burgers

Description

Here we have the famous smash burger. A simple, delicious, and mouth-watering burger to enjoy with the family. Did I also say it was super easy to make? Go to your faourite butcher, buy some beef, and simply roll them into 100g balls. Buy your favourite toppings and sauces, and of course brioche buns to hold everything together.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Brioche Buns
  • Vegetables
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Your favourite sauces

Steps

  1. Roll beef into 100g balls
  2. Heat cast iron skillet to high heat
  3. Place beef balls on skillet and press firmly until flat and thin
  4. Sprinkle spices and top side, cook for 2 minutes, flip, and repate on other side
  5. Top with cheese, prepare bun with ingredients and place your smash burder on top of some lettuce
  6. There ya have it! A smash burger in less than 15 minutes!
