Energy Oats

Description

High energy breakfast to start your day right. This delcious meal is packed with nutrients, minerals, and protein. If you like Reeses Pieces, keep reading!

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Peanut Butter
  • Natural Cacao Powder
  • Cinnamon
  • Bananas
  • Ground Flaxseed

Steps

  1. Soak oats in warm water
  2. Add a tablespoon of peanut butter and flaxseed
  3. Sprinkle some cinnamon and cacao powder
  4. Add sliced banana, and mix all ingredients together
