Curry Rice

Description

Rice, chickpeas, curry, and some grilled veggies? Yes please! Your house will smell amazing for days after cooking this delicious meal. The chickpeas add a protein punch, along with the flavourful spices.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chickpeas
  • Onions and garlic
  • Curry, salt and pepper
  • Tomatoes
  • Coconut milk

Steps

  1. Fry onions and garlic
  2. Add tomatoes and coconut milk
  3. Add spices and cooked chickpeas
  4. Mix well and simmer ingredients for 15 minutes
  5. Top on cooked rice and eat!
